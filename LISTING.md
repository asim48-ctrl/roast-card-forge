## Gumroad / Ko-fi / Itch Copy

Roast Card Forge is a browser-based roast-card generator for creators, students,
founders, and terminally online group chats.

Paste a target and a little context, choose a tone and theme, then generate a
shareable 1200 x 630 PNG card with a roast score, diagnosis, evidence,
punchline, and one useful fix disguised as mercy.

The free version includes:

- Resume roasts
- GitHub repo roasts
- LinkedIn profile roasts

- Dating profile roasts

- Copyable social caption
